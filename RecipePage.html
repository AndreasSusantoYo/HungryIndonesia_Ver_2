Soto Betawi

Food Origin: DKI JAKARTA

Recipe Approved by Soto Betawi H. Mamat

4.5 / 5

Soto Betawi originates from the Betawi people, an indigenous group in Jakarta, Indonesia's capital city. This flavorful soup is deeply rooted in the cultural heritage of the Betawi community and has become a cherished culinary tradition, representing the rich diversity of Indonesian cuisine.

  • Portion: 6 servings
  • Calories: 180-210 cal per serving
  • Prep time: 20 mins
  • Cooking time: 60 mins
