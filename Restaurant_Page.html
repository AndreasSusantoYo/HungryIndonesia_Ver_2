Soto Betawi by Soto Betawi H. Mamat

Jl. Boulevard Raya Gading Serponng No.l, Pakulonan Bar., Kec. KLP. Dua, Kabupaten Tangerang, Banten 15810

4.7 / 5

Soto Betawi H. Mamat is considered as one of the best Soto Betawi in town. Moreover, it was established in 1960 and has since
grown into a legendary eateries in Tangerang. Lots of people compete to save a seat inside this eatery to savor the perfect
combination of the meat and the coconut milk soup.
