About Us

HungryIndonesia is a website dedicated to Indonesian traditional cuisine, featuring authentic recipes from all provinces across the
country. We also aim to simplify restaurant searches for those who are interested in Indonesian traditional dishes. Our unique
approach involves providing alternative ingredients in our recipes to give users more options when cooking these dishes.

Explore the Indonesian traditional dishes you want to cook or find Indonesian restaurants to visit on HungryIndonesia.

Happy cooking and happy food hunting, HungryPeople!
